How To:
Starburst Flower

Introduction

The following instructions will take you step-by-step through the making of an origami starburst flower. It will take about 5 minutes to complete. Origami is a practice that is best served by crisp, clean folds, so pick up a piece of paper and follow along!

Tools and Supplies

The overall cost will be about $4.

  • Scissors

    A sharp pair of scissors to prune your flower!

  • Origami Paper

    1 piece of 9 cm x 9 cm origami paper.

Step 1 Fold the paper in half and then fold backwards diagonally.

Horizontally and vertically fold the square piece of paper. Flip the paper over and fold along both diagonals.

Step 2 Create a square.

The folds should make it easy to push in the corners and fold into a smaller square shape.

Step 3 Create a kite.

Lay the square in front of you and orient it with the open corner point up like a diamond.Take the right corner and fold it into the middle line. This will create a smaller triangle shape. Do this on both halves of the diamond for all four sides to create a shape resembling a kite.

Step 4 Create accordion folds within the kite.

Starting with the right side of the kite, unfold the crease you just made and crease back out. Holding the left side down, open up the right quadrant of paper and push the folds inside out. Repeat this on the left side. Then flip the shape over and repeat on the other sides. This should leave you with a layered kite shape.

Step 5 Create a triangle.

With your kite shape, turn to a solid side with none of the opposite side of the paper showing. Fold down the top triangle in line with the center fold and along the bottom of the triangle. Repeat on each side solid side. This should leave you with a triangle shape.

Step 6 Create a crease for the stem.

Fold the bottom tip of the triangle upwards to create a crease at the bottom tip of the folded down triangles from Step 5.

Step 7 Create the stem.

Fold the triangle shape in half, along the center fold. With the vertical side of the triangle on the right, fold the bottom tip up and to the left so that the top edge is horizontal.

Step 8 Open the flower.

Pinch the stem you created in Step 7 and use your other hand to push the crease in the top part of the triangle in the opposite direction. This will help the accordion-folded triangle to fan out and create the flower shape.

Step 9 Separate and shape the petals.

Separate the petals radially and shape them using your finger tips. Trim each of the petal corners to create a starburst effect.

Complete Add a stem or create a bouquet!

This type of flower is perfect for adorning the top of an origami succulent or for attaching to a stem and adding extra filler to an origami bouquet!
